NASA’s DRA version 5.0, which is their current plan for sending humans to Mars, is described in two main documents:

Human Exploration of Mars Design Reference Architecture 5.0
NASA/SP-2009-566
Mars Architecture Steering Group
NASA Headquarters
Bret G. Drake, editor
NASA Johnson Space Center, Houston, Texas
July, 2009
[PDF, 3.6MB, 100pp]

Human Exploration of Mars Design Reference Architecture 5.0 Addendum
NASA/SP-2009-566-ADD
Mars Architecture Steering Group
NASA Headquarters
Bret G. Drake, editor
NASA Johnson Space Center, Houston, Texas
July 2009
[PDF, 32.4MB, 406pp]

These slides [PDF, 1.6MB, 31pp] may also be useful in providing an overview of the DRA.

David I. Kaplan, Editor (Lyndon B. Johnson Space Center, Houston, Texas)
Lyndon B. Johnson Space Center, Houston, Texas
July 1997

Reference Mission Version 3.0 Addendum to the Human Exploration of Mars: The Reference Mission of the NASA Mars Exploration Study Team
