Mars Transit Habitat

Transit Hab

Habitat where the crew live and work while en route to and from Mars. Comprised of BA 330-DS and includes ECLSS.

Mars Surface Habitat

Surface Hab

Habitat where the crew live and work while on the surface of Mars. Comprised of BA 330-MDS and includes ISAP and ECLSS.

Rocket to carry the crew from Mars surface to Mars orbit. Includes descent stage, ISPP system, ascent stage and Pern-2.

Spaceship that carries the crew to Mars and back. Primarily comprised of the Transit Hab plus fuel tanks and engines.
