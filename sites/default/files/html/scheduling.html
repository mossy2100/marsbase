The pages in this section detail the scheduling and timing of the missions. An overall summary:

2015
  • Begin planning and design
  • Begin engaging international and commercial partners
  • Begin developing collaboration frameworks
2020-30
  • Precursor missions
  • Improve TRL of mission hardware
  • Begin selecting and training astronauts
2031
  • Alpha Mission: predeployment (Surface Hab, Rover, MCOS)
2032
  • Construct MTV-1
2033
  • Alpha Mission: crew outbound (MTV-1)
  • Bravo Mission: predeployment (Surface Hab, Rover)
2034
  • Construct MTV-2
2035
  • Alpha Mission: crew return
  • Bravo Mission: crew outbound (MTV-2)
  • Charlie Mission: predeployment (greenhouse, water miner)
2037
  • Bravo Mission: crew return
  • Charlie Mission: crew outbound (MTV-1)
  • Delta Mission: predeployment (greenhouse, water miner)
2039
  • Charlie Mission: crew return
  • Delta Mission: crew outbound (MTV-2)
  • Echo Mission: predeployment (earth-moving equipment, etc.)
2040-2100
  • Further missions and ongoing development and operation of the base
